About

Biography

Chiang-Yu Cheng is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Data Science at Soochow University, Taiwan. His research examines how generative AI systems, search interfaces, and web governance mechanisms affect visibility, accountability, and knowledge representation. He is particularly interested in the ways AI-powered systems reallocate informational exposure across websites, languages, industries, and regions.

His recent work focuses on citation governance in generative search, AI crawler governance, knowledge visibility gaps in training data, and the institutional consequences of platform-mediated discovery. Methodologically, he combines computational auditing, structured query experiments, web scraping, and empirical modeling to investigate emerging information infrastructures.
